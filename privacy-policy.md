# Privacy Policy – YKS Sıralama

**Last updated: March 2026**

YKS Sıralama respects your privacy. This application is designed to work fully offline and does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

---

## Data Collection

YKS Sıralama does not collect any personal information.

All data you enter — including TYT/AYT net scores, diploma grade, and exam year — is stored locally on your device only and used only within the current session.

---

## Data Usage

The data you enter in the app is used solely to provide core app functionality, including:

- TYT and AYT score calculation
- University ranking estimation
- Listing suitable university programs based on your score

No data is transmitted to any external server.

---

## Location & Map Usage

When you tap the map button on a university listing, the app uses your device's native Maps application (Apple Maps on iOS, Google Maps on Android) to display the university's address. The app itself does not access, store, or transmit your location data.

---

## Third-Party Services

YKS Sıralama does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

---

## Data Sharing

YKS Sıralama does not share any user data with third parties.

---

## Data Deletion

Since no data is stored persistently on your device, there is nothing to delete. All entered data is cleared when the app session ends. You may also fully remove all app data by uninstalling the application.

---

## Children's Privacy

YKS Sıralama is intended for use by high school students preparing for the YKS university entrance exam. The app does not knowingly collect any personal data from any user, including those under the age of 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected within the app or on the app's store listing.
